I graduated from the University of Tennessee - Knoxville with a B.S. in Computer Science in May of 2023. I was involved in several extracurriculars there, including the Tennessee Speech and Debate Society, the Society of Women Engineers, and the Heath Integrated Business and Engineering Program.

After graduating, I joined MathWorks as a software engineer in the Engineering Development Group before progressing into a technical documentation role on the MATLAB team.

I currently write documentation for the MATLAB software, specifically covering MATLAB's interfaces to Python and C++. I also support the Foundation Architecture (think MATLAB crash handling) and Software Performance teams.

I'm always looking for new projects to contribute to, so if you'd like to get in touch, please shoot me a message on LinkedIn.
